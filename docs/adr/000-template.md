# ADR Template

## Title

Short noun phrase describing the decision

## Status

Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by [ADR-XXX](XXX-title.md)

## Date

YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What is the issue that we're seeing that is motivating this decision or change?

Include:
- Technical story or problem
- Relevant constraints
- Alternatives considered
- Forces at play (technical, business, social)

## Decision

What is the change that we're actually proposing or have agreed to implement?

State the decision clearly and concisely. This is the "what" we will do.

Include:
- Clear statement of the decision
- Reasoning behind the choice
- Why this option was selected over alternatives

## Consequences

What becomes easier or more difficult to do because of this change?

Include:
- **Positive**: Benefits gained from this decision
- **Negative**: Drawbacks or trade-offs accepted
- **Neutral**: Side effects neither good nor bad
- **Risks**: Potential issues to monitor

## Implementation Notes

Optional section for implementation details:
- Key files/modules affected
- Configuration changes needed
- Migration steps if applicable
- Timeline considerations

## References

- Links to relevant documentation
- Related tickets/issues
- External resources
